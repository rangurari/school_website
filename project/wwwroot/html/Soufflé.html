A recipe for a soufflé in the microwave

ingridians:

  • 4 tablespoons of self-raising flour
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of cocoa
  • one egg
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of canola oil
  • 4 chocolate cubes

how to make

  1. Mix the flour, sugar and cocoa powder in the cup.
  2. Add the egg, milk and oil to the dry mixture in the cup and mix well.
  3. Place the chocolate cubes on top of the mixture.
  4. Heat the mug in the microwave on the highest power for 3 minutes.
  5. Wait another 3 minutes for the cake to set, remove from the microwave
