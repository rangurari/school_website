A recipe for chocolate chip cookies

ingridians:

  • 290 grams of very soft butter
  • 200 grams of white sugar (1 cup)
  • 200 grams of light brown sugar (1 cup)
  • 2 eggs
  • A little vanilla extract
  • 400 grams of flour (2 and a half cups plus 5 tablespoons)
  • 5 grams of baking powder (one and a half teaspoons)
  • 5 grams of salt (a little less than a teaspoon)
  • 440 grams of millk chocolate chips (2 cups)

how to make:

  1. mix the butter and both sugers until it becomes creamy
  2. add the eggs and the vanilla extract mix until it becomes creamy again
  3. add in the flour, baking powder, salt and chocolate chips and mix just until you get a uniform dough
  4. line a tray with baking paper. Immediately cut circles from the dough weighing about 40 grams each
  5. transfer the tray to the refrigerator for 2-4 hours
  6. heat an oven to 180 degrees with turbo.
  7. bake the cookies for 11 minutes, until they flatten, slightly golden
