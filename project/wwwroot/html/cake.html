A recipe for a chocolate cake

ingridians:

  • 2 1/2 cups white flour
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup of powder for making chocolate
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of canola oil
  • 2 bags of vanilla sugar
  • 2 cups of boiling water

Coating

  • 1 cup of chocolate spread
  • 1/4 cup of boiling water

how to make

  1. Heat the oven to 170 degrees.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, chocolate,
    baking powder, baking soda powder.
  3. Beat eggs with oil and vanilla sugar. Pour
    into a bowl with the flour and whisk to a smooth mixture
    while gradually adding boiling water. Pour into 3 greased
    molds and bake for about 45 minutes until the cakes are spongy
    to the touch and a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs.
  4. Prepare the coating, coat and decorate:
    mix a few tablespoons of chocolate spread with
    boiling water until the texture of
    a thick sauce. Pour over the cakes and let it partially absorb.
